The Intersection of Engineering & Human Impact

A Non-Traditional Path Built on Real-World Perspective

The journey into software engineering began with a foundational background in education, counseling, and retail operations—a unique trajectory that now defines a distinctive approach to technology. Early career experience on the retail frontline provided a deep, firsthand understanding of how digital solutions impact users in real-time environments.

From Operations to Enterprise Security

Career growth led from leadership roles into corporate security within a Global Security Operations Center. This phase focused on monitoring enterprise systems and supporting critical incident response initiatives, instilling a rigorous standard for system reliability and security.

The Foundation: Empathy, Leadership, and Development

Concurrent with a corporate path, dedicated service as an educator and counselor built a master-level expertise in communication, leadership, and human development. These core competencies serve as the blueprint for modern technical execution, ensuring that every system is built with empathy, accessibility, and user-centered thinking at its heart.

Engineering with Purpose

Today, the work operates at the vital intersection of engineering execution and human impact. The result is the delivery of robust, technically superior systems that remain deeply focused on the people they serve.
